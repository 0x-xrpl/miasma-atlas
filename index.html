01 / Core Problem
Most systems check the transaction.
MIASMA checks the memory that caused it.

AI agents do not only sign transactions. They inherit memory, policy files, tool context, previous state, and hidden instructions that can shape the action before the wallet sees anything.

Visible surfaceA wallet can show amount, recipient, and action type.
Hidden causeThe dangerous instruction can live earlier in memory, policy, or previous state.
Pre-wallet boundaryThe right place to stop it is before approval, signature, submit, or digest.
